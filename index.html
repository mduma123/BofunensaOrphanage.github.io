About The Organisation

*Bonufesa is a place of peace because we care about the future for tomorrow.


Introduction

*Bofunesa Orphan and Vulnerable children is an organisation registered under Social Development as a NPO, The Organisation was formed on the 08 March 2018 in Maboloka Rural Brits District in North West. Aim is to improve and help the youth with their education and their talents. And mentorships programme.
*And further more by working together with the community to fight the growing rate of Crime, Unemployment, Teenage pregnancy, Alcohol, Substance abuse and School drops out, through motivation using EDUCATION and ART. So that Education can be their priority or important to them.

Organisation Objectives

ORGANISATION MAIN OBJECTIVES ARE:
*To provide after school education programmes to orphans learners in the community.
*To provide learners with food in the centre during after school programmes.
*To encourage youth and orphans to study.
*To provide laundry service to orphans.
*To promote healthy living and personal hygiene in the community.
*To organize events for talents search in the community.
*To encourage and support youth and orphans to utilize their skills and talents.


ORGANIZATION’S SECONDARY OBJECTIVES WILL BE TO:
*To work in collaboration with other organizations that support or have similar objectives to strengthen and share knowledge and experience.
*To in power the community with skills and training
